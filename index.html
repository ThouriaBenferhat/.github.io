About Thouria

📖

Born in Morocco, raised in Algeria and the United States, I've lived the multilingual experience that inspires my work today.

For 36 years, I taught Arabic at the United Nations, helping diplomats and international professionals connect across languages and cultures. This experience showed me the transformative power of language learning and cultural understanding.

Now, I channel that passion into creating multilingual children's books that make language learning natural, fun, and accessible. My books feature stories in Arabic, English, French, Spanish, and Bosnian—reflecting the beautiful diversity of our world.

I also teach online, continuing my mission to build bridges between cultures through language education. Every book I write is an invitation for children to see themselves in multiple languages and cultures.
